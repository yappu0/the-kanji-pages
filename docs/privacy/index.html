The Kanji / Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how The Kanji handles information when you use the app. The Kanji is a Japanese word quiz app that stores quiz progress and app settings on your device and uses Firebase Analytics to understand aggregate product usage.

Information Stored On Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device to provide app functionality:

  • onboarding completion state
  • language preference
  • text-size preference
  • sound setting
  • daily quiz completion state
  • quiz scores, streak count, total sessions, and related progress statistics

This local information is used to show progress, streaks, settings, and daily completion state. The app does not provide account login, cloud sync, or server-side progress recovery.

Analytics

The Kanji uses Firebase Analytics to collect app usage analytics. This helps us understand how the app is used and improve app quality and product experience.

Analytics events may include product usage events such as quiz start, quiz completion, feature usage, screen usage, app version, device model, operating-system version, language or region settings, and approximate usage timing.

The current analytics implementation does not intentionally send user identity, account information, advertising identifiers, raw question text, raw answer text, payment information, contact information, or session replay recordings.

Firebase Analytics is provided by Google Firebase. Google may process analytics data according to Firebase and Google terms and privacy practices.

Information We Do Not Collect

The current app does not collect or upload:

  • account registration information
  • login credentials, name, email address, or profile information
  • payment or purchase information
  • user-generated content
  • cloud-synced quiz progress
  • advertising display data from an ad SDK

Device Permissions

The current app does not request access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, photo library, or app tracking permission.

Purchases And Ads

The current app does not include in-app purchases, subscriptions, paywalls, purchase restoration, or advertising display SDKs.

Children

The Kanji is a general Japanese word quiz app. It does not include account creation, social features, direct messaging, or user-generated content.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes, including changes to analytics, purchases, ads, crash reporting, account features, cloud sync, or other network services. The updated policy will be published at this URL.
