Supported Features

The Kanji is a compact Japanese word-discovery quiz app. The current app supports:

  • a daily 10-question quiz
  • practice collections for words, idioms, sayings, and number memory
  • short feedback and explanations after answering quiz questions
  • local progress, including streak, total sessions, accuracy, and recent daily results
  • display language choices: System, English, and Japanese
  • text-size choices
  • sound-effect preference
  • About and license information inside Settings
  • Firebase Analytics for app usage analytics

The current app does not provide account login, cloud sync, server backup, leaderboards, subscription purchases, or in-app purchases.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my progress synced across devices?

No. Progress and settings are stored locally on the device. The app currently does not provide account login, cloud sync, or server-side progress recovery.

Can I restore progress after deleting or reinstalling the app?

The app does not provide its own account-based restore feature. Any restore behavior depends on the device and operating-system backup settings. The app cannot recover progress from a server account.

How do I change the app language?

Open Settings in the app and choose one of the language options:

  • System
  • English
  • Japanese

The System option follows the device language when that language is supported by the app.

How do I change text size?

Open Settings in the app and choose the preferred text size. The current app offers Standard, Large, and Extra Large text-size choices.

How do I turn sound effects on or off?

Open Settings in the app and use the Sound effects switch.

Why did my daily quiz already show as completed?

The daily quiz is designed as a daily habit loop. After completing the daily quiz for the day, you can continue using the practice collections.

Does the app include audio, handwriting, flashcards, AI tutoring, or JLPT exam preparation?

No. The current app does not include those features.
